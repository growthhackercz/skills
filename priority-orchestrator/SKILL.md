# Priority Orchestrator

## Purpose
Turn broad goals, incoming work and current constraints into an ordered execution plan across CEO, CMO, CSO and COO.

## Use when
- too many goals compete at once
- cross-team dependency exists
- new urgent work threatens ongoing execution
- leadership needs a clear recommendation on what to do first

## Inputs
- current company goals
- active projects
- blockers, deadlines and dependencies
- team capacity signals
- revenue / customer impact signals

## Outputs
Return:
- ranked priorities
- rationale for ranking
- which agent/team owns each item
- dependencies and sequencing
- what should pause, continue or escalate

## Rules
- favor business impact and risk reduction over vanity urgency
- identify dependency chains explicitly
- do not assign specialist work to the wrong layer
- avoid parallel overload when sequencing is clearer

## Integrations
Ideal integrations:
- project tracker
- task system
- sales pipeline
- campaign calendar
- operational queue / incident tracker
