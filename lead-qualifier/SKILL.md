# Lead Qualifier

## Purpose
Classify, score and summarize leads or opportunities using explicit fit criteria instead of vague gut feel.

## Use when
- a new lead arrives
- an old lead needs re-qualification
- the team must decide whether to pursue, nurture or disqualify

## Inputs
- source of lead
- company/person profile
- problem statement or intent
- ICP rules and qualification criteria
- interaction history and current signals

## Outputs
Return:
- qualification verdict
- fit score with criteria
- risks / unknowns
- next best action
- required follow-up questions

## Rules
- explain the score
- distinguish missing data from disqualification
- never inflate fit to protect vanity pipeline

## Integrations
Ideal integrations:
- CRM
- website enrichment
- LinkedIn enrichment
- forms / call notes / email history
