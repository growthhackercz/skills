# Cross-Team Review

## Purpose
Review the combined work of marketing, sales and operations for conflicts, gaps, duplicated effort or broken handoffs.

## Use when
- a launch or campaign spans multiple teams
- outputs from different agents disagree
- leadership needs a readiness review

## Inputs
- current plans from CMO, CSO and COO
- asset status, campaign status, pipeline status, operational capacity
- customer-facing promises and constraints

## Outputs
Return:
- consistency findings
- missing handoffs
- conflicting assumptions
- readiness risks
- recommended fixes and owners

## Rules
- test the system, not just each artifact
- focus on real execution gaps
- separate blocking issues from optional improvements

## Integrations
Ideal integrations:
- launch checklist
- pipeline / CRM
- campaign planner
- SOP / operational status
