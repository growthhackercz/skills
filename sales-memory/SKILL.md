# Sales Memory

## Purpose
Maintain structured continuity of commercial context across leads, opportunities and customers.

## Use when
- a sales agent needs history
- a new interaction starts
- deal context is scattered across channels or tools

## Inputs
- interaction summaries
- objections, promises, next steps
- opportunity stage updates
- account details and product relevance

## Outputs
Return or store:
- compact account/opportunity memory
- critical commercial facts
- unresolved questions
- recommended reminders for future touchpoints

## Rules
- capture only useful business context
- record promises and next steps precisely
- separate observed facts from interpretations

## Integrations
Ideal integrations:
- CRM
- email and call logs
- meeting notes
- proposal history
