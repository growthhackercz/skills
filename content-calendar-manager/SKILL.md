# Content Calendar Manager

## Purpose
Maintain a realistic, strategically ordered content calendar tied to campaigns, launches and distribution capacity.

## Use when
- content is produced ad hoc
- publishing needs sequencing
- the team needs visibility over upcoming content work

## Inputs
- campaign plan
- publishing channels
- production capacity
- launch dates
- priority themes

## Outputs
Return:
- calendar proposal
- publishing sequence
- production dependencies
- bottleneck warnings
- required approvals

## Rules
- calendar must reflect capacity
- tie content to goals, not just volume
- identify missing assets or approvals early

## Integrations
Ideal integrations:
- project/task tracker
- campaign planner
- publisher workflows
